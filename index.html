REVIVAL

The history of Revival is beyond any human understanding. But at a high level: Once upon a time, a group of young scientists from the South Brunswick High School (SBHS) did a science experiment creating a revival pill. In theory, whoever takes the pill will instantly know whether they are inherently a dog person or a cat person. While the scientists were crafting the pill, the chemical exploded and spread worldwide, turning people into Doggo or Catto. No one has been physically hurt, but mentally. This instantly made people realize their inherent animal and the rest of the story continues...
